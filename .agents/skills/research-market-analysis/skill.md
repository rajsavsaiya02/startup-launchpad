# Goal
To provide the product team with data-driven insights into market trends, competitor strategies, and potential gaps in the startup ecosystem.

# Step-by-Step Logic
1. **Define Research Scope**: Identify specific questions (e.g., "What are the common fee structures for startup accelerators?").
2. **Competitive Landscape**: List top 3-5 direct and indirect competitors.
3. **Deep Dive Search**: Use advanced search operators (site:, filetype:, intitle:) to find whitepapers, financial reports, and user reviews.
4. **Data Synthesis**: Categorize findings into SWOT (Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, Threats) analysis.
5. **Actionable Insights**: Translate research data into specific feature recommendations for Startup LaunchPad.

# Technical Constraints
- **Freshness**: Data must be from the last 12-24 months.
- **Verification**: Cross-reference facts from at least two reputable sources.

# Code Patterns
```markdown
# Market Research Query Template:
- "competitor_name" pricing structure 2024
- best startup launchpad platforms for "niche_market"
- user pain points in "competitor_name" reviews
- "niche_market" startup funding trends 2024 filetype:pdf
```

# Tool Integration
- **Google Search**: Primary research engine. Use Google Trends for interest-over-time analysis.
- **Figma**: Use research findings to inform UI/UX personas and user flow decisions.
- **Doc-Technical-Writing**: Incorporate detailed research findings into formal Market Analysis Reports.
